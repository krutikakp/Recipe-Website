Delicious Pohe Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 potato, boiled and diced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Wash the poha under running water until they become soft. Drain excess water and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. When they crackle, add cumin seeds and curry leaves.
  3. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saute until onions turn translucent.
  4. Add boiled potatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Now add the washed poha and gently mix everything together.
  6. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with lemon wedges.
