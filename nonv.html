Anda Curry

Maharashtrian Anda Curry is a flavorful egg curry dish originating from the Indian state of Maharashtra. It typically consists of hard-boiled eggs cooked in a spicy tomato-based gravy enriched with aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. Onions, garlic, and ginger are often used as the base for the curry, providing depth of flavor. The dish is usually garnished with fresh coriander leaves and served hot with rice or Indian breads like chapati or paratha. It's a popular and comforting dish enjoyed across Maharashtra and beyond.

Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a traditional Maharashtrian dish made with chicken pieces cooked in a flavorful gravy. It typically includes a blend of spices such as turmeric, chili powder, coriander, and garam masala, along with onions, tomatoes, and sometimes coconut milk for richness. This dish is known for its bold flavors and is often served with rice or bread, making it a hearty and satisfying meal.

Kolambi Fry

Kolambi Fry is a popular dish from the state of Maharashtra, India. It consists of prawns marinated in a blend of spices such as red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and sometimes garlic and ginger paste. The marinated prawns are then shallow-fried until they are crispy and golden brown. Kolambi Fry is often served as a starter or appetizer in Maharashtrian cuisine, and it is known for its flavorful and spicy taste.

Egg Pulav

Maharashtra Egg Pulav is a popular rice dish from the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is a flavorful and aromatic preparation made by cooking rice with boiled eggs and a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, cloves, cinnamon, and bay leaves. Onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and green chilies are sautéed to form the base of the dish, which is then mixed with the boiled eggs and rice. The dish is typically garnished with fresh coriander leaves and served hot, often accompanied by raita or yogurt on the side. It's a satisfying and wholesome meal enjoyed by many in Maharashtra and beyond.

Malvani Chicken Sukka

Malvani Chicken Sukka is a traditional dish from the Malvan region of Maharashtra, India. It's a flavorful and spicy chicken dish cooked with a blend of aromatic spices such as coriander, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, and red chili powder. The dish typically involves marinating chicken pieces in a mixture of spices, coconut, and tamarind paste, then cooking them until tender and coated with a thick, rich gravy. It's often served as a main course with rice or bread and is popular for its bold flavors and comforting warmth.

Kolhapuri Chicken

Kolhapuri Chicken is a spicy and aromatic dish originating from the Kolhapur region of Maharashtra, India. It's known for its fiery red color and intense flavors. The dish features chicken cooked in a rich gravy made from a blend of flavorful spices such as Kashmiri red chili powder, garam masala, coriander, cumin, and black pepper. Additionally, the use of ingredients like garlic, ginger, onions, and tomatoes adds depth to the dish. It's typically served with rice or bread and is beloved for its bold and robust taste.
